Password Security Tips

Length Matters

Use at least 12 characters. Longer passwords are exponentially harder to crack.

Randomness is Key

Avoid patterns, dictionary words, or personal information in your passwords.

Unique Per Account

Never reuse passwords across different accounts. Each account needs its own unique password.

Regular Updates

Change your passwords regularly, especially for sensitive accounts like banking and email.
