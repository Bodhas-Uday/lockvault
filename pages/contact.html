Frequently Asked Questions

Is LockVault free to use?

Yes! LockVault is completely free to use. We believe that password security should be accessible to everyone.

How secure is my data?

Your data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption and stored locally in your browser. We never have access to your passwords.

Can I sync across devices?

Currently, data is stored locally. We're working on cloud sync functionality for future releases.

What if I forget my master password?

Unfortunately, we cannot recover your master password as it's never stored on our servers. Please keep it safe!

Can I export my passwords?

Yes! You can export all your passwords from the Profile page in JSON format for backup purposes.

Do you offer enterprise solutions?

We're currently focused on individual users, but enterprise features are planned for future releases.
